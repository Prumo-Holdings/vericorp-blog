Welcome to the VeriCorp developer blog.

We built VeriCorp to solve a real problem: verifying European companies is fragmented across dozens of national registries, each with different formats, authentication methods, and data models. Our API unifies all of this behind a single endpoint.

## What to expect

This blog will cover:

- **API design decisions** — why we chose certain patterns and trade-offs
- **EU compliance deep dives** — VAT validation, eInvoicing (Peppol), KYB requirements
- **Technical tutorials** — integrating company verification into your fintech stack
- **Performance engineering** — how we keep response times under 200ms across 27 EU registries

## For developers, by developers

Every post includes working code examples. No marketing fluff, no "synergy" — just practical engineering content for backend developers building in the EU fintech space.

If you're building something that needs company verification, [check out our API docs](https://vericorp.dev/docs).

Stay tuned for our first technical deep dive.
